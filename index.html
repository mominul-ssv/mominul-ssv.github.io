Publications

CosSIF: Cosine similarity-based image filtering algorithm to overcome low inter-class variation in synthetic medical image datasets

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), when trained using medical image datasets, often produce synthetic images that bear resemblance to images from other classes. This research seeks to tackle this issue by introducing an image filtering algorithm called Cosine Similarity-based Image Filtering (CosSIF). Furthermore, two distinct filtering approaches are proposed: Filtering Before GAN Training (FBGT) and Filtering After GAN Training (FAGT), both of which leverage the CosSIF algorithm. The primary objective of the FBGT and FAGT methodologies is to enhance GANs' capacity to generate synthetic images showcasing highly discriminative features, while also eliminating synthetic images that closely resemble those from other classes.
